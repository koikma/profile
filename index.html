Who We Are

Dedicated to the Growth
of Malaysia's Cooperatives

Koperasi Warga Institut Koperasi Malaysia Berhad (KoIKMa) is a premier cooperative institution dedicated to advancing the capabilities, governance, and sustainability of cooperatives across Malaysia. As a trusted partner to cooperative organisations nationwide, we deliver transformative training programmes, strategic consultation, and capacity-building initiatives that elevate cooperative performance to international standards.

Founded on the principles of unity, transparency, and shared prosperity, KoIKMa serves as a bridge between cooperative aspirations and measurable outcomes. Our multidisciplinary team of experts brings decades of combined experience in cooperative law, financial management, leadership development, and organisational strategy.

Recognised by the relevant authorities and aligned with national cooperative development frameworks, KoIKMa is committed to nurturing a generation of cooperative leaders who are equipped to navigate an evolving economic landscape while staying true to cooperative values.
